Introduction

Pollution is the unwanted thing in our environment. Pollution is the introduction of substance(or energy) that causes adverse changes in the environment and living entities.

Pollution need not always be caused by chemical substances such as particulates (like smoke and dust). Forms of energy such as sound, heat, light can also cause Pollution.

Source: fao.org
